PRIVACY POLICY

Path of the Wisp

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Path of the Wisp (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is operated by DappGames. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Path of the Wisp mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically by the App or third-party services integrated into it:

Device Information

  • Device model
  • Operating system version
  • Device language
  • IP address (via third-party services)
  • Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android), where permitted

Usage Data

  • Gameplay progress
  • Scores and achievements
  • Session duration
  • Feature usage

Performance Data

  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostic information
  • Error reports

Approximate Location

  • Coarse location derived from IP address for advertising purposes

1.2 Information You Provide

We may process:

  • In-app purchase history (processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
  • Subscription status (such as VIP access)
  • Communications sent to us via email

We do not collect or store payment card information.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Save game progress locally on your device
  • Validate in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Diagnose bugs and improve performance
  • Deliver advertisements (personalized where consent is provided)
  • Analyze aggregated usage trends

Core gameplay functionality does not require personalized advertising consent.

3. Third-Party Services

The App integrates third-party services that may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

Unity Ads

Used to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (with App Tracking Transparency consent on iOS where applicable)
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location

Unity’s Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

Apple App Store / Google Play Store

All purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple or Google. We do not access, process, or store payment information.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we request permission before accessing the Advertising Identifier (IDFA). You may:

  • Allow Tracking (personalized ads)
  • Ask App Not to Track (non-personalized ads)

You can change this at any time in: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

5. Data Storage

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device
  • We do not operate our own external gameplay servers
  • Data processed by third-party SDKs is handled under their respective policies
  • Deleting the App removes locally stored data

6. Children’s Privacy

Path of the Wisp is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such data has been collected, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent to tracking
  • Object to certain data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Data Retention

  • We retain data only as necessary:
  • Local game progress: until the App is deleted
  • Crash and analytics data: retained by third-party providers per their policies
  • Purchase records: managed by Apple or Google

9. International Transfers

Third-party providers (such as Unity Ads) may process data in countries outside your own, including the United States, subject to appropriate safeguards.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the “Last Updated” date.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
