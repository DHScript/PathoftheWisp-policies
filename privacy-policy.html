📄 PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Firefly Connect ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Firefly Connect mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. By using Firefly Connect, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, device language, IP address (via third-party services), Advertising Identifier (IDFA on iOS, Advertising ID on Android) — only with your permission
  • Usage Data: Gameplay statistics, levels completed, scores and achievements, session duration, feature usage
  • Performance Data: Crash logs, diagnostic information, loading times, error reports
  • Approximate Location: Coarse location derived from IP address (used for advertising purposes)

1.2 Information You Provide

We may collect:

  • In-app purchase history (processed by Apple App Store or Google Play Store)
  • Subscription status (VIP or premium access)
  • Optional support communications (if you email us)

We do not collect or store your payment card information.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Save game progress locally on your device
  • Validate in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Improve gameplay experience
  • Fix bugs and optimize performance
  • Deliver personalized advertisements (with your consent)
  • Analyze usage trends

Core gameplay does not require personalized advertising consent.

3. Third-Party Services

Firefly Connect integrates third-party services that may collect data:

Unity Ads

Used to display advertisements. Unity Ads may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (with ATT consent on iOS)
  • Device information
  • Ad interaction data
  • Approximate location

Unity's data handling is governed by Unity's Privacy Policy.

Apple App Store / Google Play Store

All purchases and subscriptions are processed through Apple or Google. We do not access or store payment information.

4. App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS devices, we request permission before accessing your Advertising Identifier (IDFA). You may:

  • Allow Tracking (personalized ads)
  • Ask App Not to Track (non-personalized ads)

You may change this anytime in: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

5. Data Storage

  • Game progress is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • We do not operate our own external game servers
  • Data transmitted through third-party SDKs is governed by those providers
  • Deleting the App removes locally stored data

6. Children's Privacy

Firefly Connect is designed for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that such information has been collected, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent to tracking
  • Object to certain data processing

You may contact us at the email below to exercise these rights.

8. Data Retention

  • Local game progress: until the App is deleted
  • Crash reports and analytics: retained by third-party providers per their policies
  • Purchase records: managed by Apple/Google

9. International Data Transfers

Third-party providers (such as Unity Ads) may process data in countries outside your own, including the United States. These providers implement appropriate safeguards.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the App after updates constitutes acceptance.
